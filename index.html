About Counsel

A practice built on being reachable.

Sandesh Kadam is an advocate practising before the Bombay High Court and the courts of Mumbai, representing clients in family, civil, cyber, succession and labour matters.

Legal disputes rarely arrive on schedule. A client may need a heirship certificate to claim what is rightfully theirs, an urgent response to a cyber fraud, or simply someone who returns their calls during a custody dispute. This practice is built around that reality: direct access to your advocate, plain explanations of where your matter stands, and preparation that respects the court's time as much as yours.
